Year 2 - Patient and Community Problem 1

Year 2, Trimester 1

Patient and Community Problem 1

Handout 1

You have gone swimming at Taurama beach, with another medical student and a science student, both friends from your first year at the University. Just as you are settling down to have lunch, you notice that someone seems to be in difficulties in the water. The science student is a good swimmer, so he dives into the water to try to help. He emerges about 10 minutes later, dragging another man out onto the wet sand, just above the waterline. The rescuer is clearly weak and exhausted and says, "it's your turn to help now, he doesn't seem to be breathing".

Year 2, Trimester 1

Patient and Community Problem 1

Learning Issues

  1. Why may he have stopped breathing?
  2. What are the consequences of cessation of respiration?
  3. What are the principles of first aid?
  4. What factors cause, or contribute to, swimming accidents?
